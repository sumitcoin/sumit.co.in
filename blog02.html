Khama Ghani - Delhi >> Jaipur Two Day’s Trip

Delhi - Jaipur, Feb 25, 2020

This time we went to Jaipur and as suggested lots of members on #001 post we have taken commercial car (CNG) for this trip which was arranged by @mapotrip and the complete trip total cost is INR 6400 including (toll, parking, road tax etc and the total KM was covered during this two day trip was 540 at the cost of INR 9.5 for per KM) services of @mapotrip is excellent. The place we cover during this trip was:


Day One: Amer Fort & Birla Temple


Day Two: Hawa Mahal, Jantar Mantar, Citi Palace, Jal Mahal, Jaigarh Fort and Nahargarh Fort We started our Journey from Delhi at 7:00AM and reached Amer Fort at 12:30PM and it takes almost 3 hours to explore Amer fort. As an engineer I loved the architecture of complete fort you should must visit to view the great architecture. After Amer fort we moved for Birla Temple and spend there more than two hours by sitting on marble and I realized the power of white stone you will not believe our body got completely relaxed.


Day two journey started with Hawa Mahal and almost taken one hours to explore to explore complete hawa Mahal every floor is having awesome architecture and great engineering work.


Jantar Mantar: It’s 400 meter from Hawa mahal mahal and it will take only 30 to 40 minutes to explore this place and same with city place.


Jal Mahal: we have spend almost 1 hours at Jal mahal and realize on the name of faith people are doing lots of mistakes. When we reached Jal Mahal a small girl came and ask to buy some small pieces of chapattis for fish but I refused to buy. Check’s pic than you will understand why I refused the same. I realize that there should be some guidelines for tourist & travelers, So that places will not get worst.


Jaigarh Fort: Also called Cheel ka Teela and its complete story of Jaivana Cannon. It’s takes 30 Minutes to explore the fort.


Nahargarh Fort: It will take lots of time to explore complete fort but I will suggest explore the same whatever time its take’s. From Nahargarh you will able to see Complete Jaipur and Amer fort.


We didn’t visit any other places due to shortage of time and we have started our journey towards Delhi. Say No To Plastic Bottle or any other plastic products.
